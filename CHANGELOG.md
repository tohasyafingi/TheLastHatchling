## [0.1.0] - 2025-01-XX

### Added
- Initial project setup with Godot 4
- Player movement and jump mechanics
- Enemy AI with patrol behavior
- Collectible crystal system
- Level design with platforming challenges
- Health and damage system
- Background music and sound effects
- Game UI with health bar and score display
- Splash screen with auto-advance (3 seconds)
- Home menu with Start/Quit options
- Level complete/game over screens
- Camera follow system

### Features
- **Player Mechanics**
  - Smooth 2D movement (left/right)
  - Jump with air control
  - Attack with Space key
  - Health system with healing items
  - Death and respawn

- **Enemy System**
  - Patrol AI
  - Collision damage
  - Visual indication of danger

- **Collectibles**
  - Crystal items for score
  - Health bottles for healing
  - Visual and audio feedback

- **Level Design**
  - Tilemap-based level 1 (Grasslands)
  - Multiple platforms and gaps
  - Strategic enemy placement
  - Decorative elements and parallax background

- **Audio System**
  - Background music
  - Jump sound
  - Attack sound
  - Enemy hit sound
  - Death sound
  - Collection sound

- **UI System**
  - Health bar
  - Pause screen
  - Game over screen
  - Level complete screen

### Technical
- Built with Godot 4.0+
- GDScript for all game logic
- Modular scene structure
- Proper signal system
- Input handling
